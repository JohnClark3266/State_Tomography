State Tomography & Active Learning

in Superconducting Quantum Systems

Theory, Implementation & Optimization

1. State Tomography: 意义与原理

Why State Tomography?

  • 目标: 重构未知的量子态密度矩阵 $\rho$。
  • 重要性:
    • Gate Fidelity: 验证逻辑门操作精度
    • QEC: 纠错编码校准与错误诊断
    • Verification: 验证纠缠与非经典性

原理: 密度矩阵重构

量子态完全由 $\rho$ 描述。对于单 Qubit:

$$ \rho = \frac{1}{2} (I + \vec{r} \cdot \vec{\sigma}) = \frac{1}{2} \begin{pmatrix} 1+\langle Z\rangle & \langle X\rangle-i\langle Y\rangle \\ \langle X\rangle+i\langle Y\rangle & 1-\langle Z\rangle \end{pmatrix} $$

极大似然估计 (MLE):

因实验噪声，直接线性反演可能产生非物理态 ($\lambda_i < 0$)。MLE 寻找最可能的合法态：

$$ \rho_{\text{MLE}} = \underset{\rho \ge 0, \text{Tr}(\rho)=1}{\text{argmax}} \mathcal{L}(\rho | \{M_i\}) $$

2. Wigner Function: 相空间可视化

物理定义: 准概率分布 (Quasi-probability Distribution) $$ W(x,p) = \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{2ipy} \langle x-y | \rho | x+y \rangle dy $$

物理意义: $W(x,p) < 0$ 的区域是非经典性 (Non-classicality) 的直接指纹。知道 Wigner 函数等价于知道密度矩阵 $\rho$。

3. 超导量子系统中的实现

系统架构

  • Cavity (存储): 高 Q 值模式
  • Qubit (探针): 辅助测量
  • Dispersive Interaction: $\chi a^\dagger a \sigma_z$

Direct Wigner Tomography

$$ W(\alpha) = \frac{2}{\pi} \text{Tr}[ D^\dagger(\alpha) \rho D(\alpha) \Pi ] $$

核心: 测量相空间位置 $\alpha$ 处的宇称 (Parity) $\Pi$

Why? $$ \Pi(\alpha) = D(\alpha) \Pi D^\dagger(\alpha) $$ 相空间平移后的宇称算符期望值对应于 Wigner 函数在该点的值： $$ W(\alpha) = \frac{2}{\pi} \langle \Pi(\alpha) \rangle $$

实验脉冲序列 (Pulse Sequence)

  1. Displace:
    对腔施加高斯脉冲 -> $D(-\alpha)$
  2. Parity Measurement (Ramsey):
    利用 Qubit 测量腔光子数奇偶性 $\langle \Pi \rangle = \langle (-1)^n \rangle$
    Qubit X90 -> Delay(1/2χ) -> Qubit X90 -> Readout
通过遍历 $\alpha$ (Raster Scan)，点对点测量重构整个 $W(x,p)$。时间成本 $O(N^2)$。

4. 实验挑战与 Machine Learning

Experimental Problems (Math)

1. Sampling Complexity
Full tomography requires $O(d^4)$ or $O(1/\epsilon^2)$ measurements.
$$ N_{meas} \gg \text{dim}(\mathcal{H})^2 $$
2. Noise & Drift
Data isn't static.
$$ y(t) = \text{Tr}(\rho \Pi_x) + \xi(t) + \delta(t)_{\text{drift}} $$
3. Non-Physicality
Linear inversion yields $\rho$ with:
$$ \lambda_{min}(\rho_{inv}) < 0 \quad (\text{Not PSD}) $$

ML Solutions

1. Active Learning (稀疏采样)
Query points with max uncertainty.
$$ x^* = \underset{x}{\text{argmax}} \ \text{Var}[ \mathcal{M}_{\theta}(x) ] $$
2. Supervised Denoising
Train NN to map noisy traces to state.
$$ \theta^* = \underset{\theta}{\text{argmin}} \ \mathbb{E}[ || f_\theta(y) - \rho_{true} ||^2 ] $$
3. Neural Density Operators
Parameterize using Cholesky factor $L$.
$$ \rho_{\theta} = \frac{L_\theta^\dagger L_\theta}{\text{Tr}(L_\theta^\dagger L_\theta)} \ge 0 $$
